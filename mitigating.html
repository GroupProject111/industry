Playlist on how to excel in remote learning

  1. Get Organised

    Becoming organised is not an easy task especially during a pandemic and the necessity of meeting academic requirements. To manage your time and incorporate mandatory habits, you must implore a time management scheduele for the time you have, great examples of such can be

    Notion- Web Organiser Asana- Online Planner

  2. Stay safe!

    It is absolutely important to adhere to the governments COVID regulations, this places both yourself and others at lesser risk of getting covid thus preventing delays in your online learning

  3. Study Ahead

    As restricting as these conditions may be, if you are fortunate enough to be free of responsibilities, a great advantage of this pandemic is that you shall have a lot more free time and can seize the opportunity to be ahead in work. All the conent shall be readily available for you online in comparison to in person where you cannot revisit learnt content. Except from your notes of course
