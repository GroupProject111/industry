Security Issues

Issues around privacy in business information systems

While technology and security mechanisms are improving with time, perpetrators of computer-related criminal acts are becoming increasingly clever to combat these mechanisms.

Therefore, it is important to recognise and understand security issues themselves.

Weak Passwords

Without a strong password, it is very easy to gain unauthorised access to a business' device and data.

Phishing Attacks

Phishing attacks can result in a loss of money, loss of intellectual property, damage to the business' reputation, and disruption of operational activities.
